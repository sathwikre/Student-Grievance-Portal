Help & FAQ

  • How do I submit a complaint?
    Go to the "New Complaint" tab, fill out the form, and click "Submit Complaint".
  • How do I view my complaints?
    Check the "Dashboard" or "My Complaints" tab for all your submitted complaints and their status.
  • How do I update my profile photo?
    Click your profile photo at the top of the dashboard to upload a new image.
  • Who can see my complaints?
    Only authorized admins and yourself can view your complaints.
  • Need more help?
    Contact your college grievance cell or email support@college.edu.
