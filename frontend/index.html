About the Portal

Learn how our platform helps students resolve issues effectively

Streamlined Grievance Resolution

The Student Grievance Portal is designed to help students submit grievances regarding academics, hostel facilities, library services, and other concerns in a streamlined and efficient manner.

Our platform ensures timely resolution by connecting students directly with the relevant authorities, providing transparency throughout the process, and enabling real-time tracking of complaint status.

With a user-friendly interface and robust backend support, we're committed to making grievance resolution faster, fairer, and more accessible for all students.
